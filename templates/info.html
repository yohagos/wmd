            This is an web-based prototype as an platform for knowledge management.
            So that all Students and Professors of the Frankfurt University of Applied Science,
            can share knowledge and interesting topics. The idea was to create something equally useful
            as research gate.

            Legal information:
            The operators of the WMD platform are not liable for misuse of the scientific papers uploaded here.
            Only users with the corresponding authorizations are able to upload new entries.
            These users are obliged to obtain the legally binding consent of the authors before
            uploading the document.

            During registration, the following data is required:
            - Username
            - Email
            - Usertype
            - Password

            The data will only be used for registration, login and new entries.
            This information is not passed on tothird parties.
